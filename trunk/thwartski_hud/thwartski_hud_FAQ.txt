//-------------------------------------------------------------------
//				2. HOW DO I CHANGE MY SCOREBOARD?
//-------------------------------------------------------------------

32-player scoreboard is used by default. 

To use a smaller one, go to \RESOURCE\THWARTSKI_SCOREBOARDS\ to find 
the version of ScoreBoard.res you want, and then paste it into the 
\RESOURCE\UI\ folder, overwriting the file. 
	
//-------------------------------------------------------------------
//		 3. THERE'S A BUTTON BLOCKING MY BACKPACK/MOTD!
//-------------------------------------------------------------------

If you want to use the html motd (most likely to look at someone's backpack), 
you can set "cl_hud_minmode 1" in your console or autoexec. 

Minmode moves the button from the center of the screen to the bottom where it's 
out of the way of the html. 

That's the only thing Thwartski Hud ever uses minmode for.
